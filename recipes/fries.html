Fried Rice Recipe

A Fully Prepared Ghanaian Fried Rice

Fried Rice is a continentally admired food

It is the very tasty and doesnt cost a lot to prepare

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Chicken
  • Oil

Steps to Prepare

  1. Cook the rice
  2. Add the cooked rice into a pot of boiling oil to fry it
  3. Stir well and cook chicken and also add to the frying rice
