Banku Recipe

A Fully Prepared Ghanaian Banku

Banku is mostly eaten by a country in Western Africa called Ghana

It is the favorite of majority of the people

Ingredients

  • Corn Dough
  • Cassava Dough
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps to Prepare

  1. Mash the cassava dough into water
  2. Add the corn dough into the mashed cassava dough in the water
  3. Add a bit of salt
  4. Put on fire and stir to solution until hard and continue stirring
