Gob3 Recipe

A Fully Prepared Ghanaian Gob3

Gob3 is a Ghanaian meal

It is the favorite of the most Ghanaian youth

Ingredients

  • Beans
  • Gari
  • Oil
  • Ripe Plantain

Steps to Prepare

  1. Add water on a cooking pot
  2. Cook the Beans
  3. Add the Gari and Stir afte the beasn is well cooked
  4. Fry the ripe plantain with vegetable oil
